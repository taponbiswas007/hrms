Instructions for Final Assessment:

The last step of the process is the Final Assessment of your performance for the year, which allows your Supervisor and you to have a constructive discussion around what you were able to accomplish successfully during the year, and where you may have fallen short of expectation. This process involves the following steps:

  • A self-assessment done by you, of your goals (without assigning any rating to them), explaining what you believe you did well, and where you may have fallen short of expectation.
  • Your Supervisor summarizes the final performance results under each dimension goals, competencies and contribution to team performance and using the bell-curve norms provided by the HR, evaluates and proposes final performance outcome rating for each team member to the second level supervisor for consent.
  • This will be followed by a performance discussion between your Supervisor and you, where the feedback and ratings on each goal; competency and the final performance outcome is shared with employee by the Supervisor, and both acknowledge the satisfactory completion of the process by signing the PMR Form.
  • The entire process will conclude with your Supervisor sharing your final performance outcome with HR. Your increment and special award will be decided and finalized at this stage.
