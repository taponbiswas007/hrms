Goal Setting

Instructions for Goal Setting:

  • The Performance Management process for IDF starts with the goal-setting process, which should be completed as per the timeline. The following steps should have been completed during this process.
  • Please begin by filling in the section on Goals in discussion with your Supervisor. Please select your goals in a way that reflects all the work that you do. You may fill between 3 to 5 goals for the year, ensuring that each goal has a weightage of between 10%-35%, and that all goals total up to 100%. These weightages simply help determine the relative importance of goals and will not be used for any scoring purposes.
  • Next, please read through and understand the section on Competencies. Competencies are the key behavioural expectations from you and are an important part of your overall performance. You have 5 competencies that have been pre-determined for the year, and all of them are equally important.
  • Finally, read and understand the expectations from you around contributing to Team/Organizational Performance. These are uniform for all members of IDF and are an important aspect of your overall performance.
